Galway

Galway Jam Circle is an easy-going, mostly acoustic jam, playing all sorts of songs ranging from Folk to Rock to Blues to Americana and even touching on Jazz from time to time. We meet weekly in Galway.

When
Every Saturday from 2pm – 6pm
Where
Galway Jam Circle, Áras na nGael, Dominic Street, Galway
