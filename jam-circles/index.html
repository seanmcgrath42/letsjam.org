Our Jam Circles

We currently run jam circles weekly in Galway & Clare with more circles opening up in each county.

Galway

An easy-going, mostly acoustic jam, playing all sorts of songs ranging from Folk to Rock to Blues to Americana and even touching on Jazz. Every Saturday from 2pm – 6pm.

Clare

Join us in the Copper Jug Cafe from 6pm to 9pm. Every Monday in Ennis, Co. Clare.
